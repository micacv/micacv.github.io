About

Hello! My name is Milica Cvrkota. I’m originally from Serbia, and I am currently based in Stuttgart, Germany, where I am working as Backend Cloud Software Engineer at IBM. In December 2020, I completed my BA degree in Computer Science at Grinnell College in Iowa. I developed my interests in computing in High School, where I prospered my problem-solving skills and was introduced to functional programming for the first time. My ambition is to make a positive impact through my work and to work towards improving the underrepresentation of women in computing. I also enjoy hiking, snowboarding, traveling, and since recently, cooking in my free time.
